Smoothies

Description

A refreshing and tasty drink for when you crave the delicousness of bananas and peanut butter

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • Â½ cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps-

  1. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  2. Enjoy!
